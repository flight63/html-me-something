Angeles City, Philippines

I was born in Angeles City, Philippines. I lived there until I was 19. Then, my family and I immigrated to the US, specifically in New Jersey.

Metuchen, NJ

Some 8500 miles later, my family and I settled in Metuchen, NJ. Metuchen is a beautiful, quiet, quaint suburb in Central New Jersey.

It is situated forty minutes away from NYC (by train or car), and the Jersey Shore beaches are only twenty minutes south. Metuchen is a great town to raise a family.

Columbia University in New York City

I immediately applied to different colleges in America, and got accepted into Columbia University's Economics-Mathematics undergraduate program in the Fall of 2004 and graduated with a bachelor's degree four years later.

Deerfield Beach, FL

After college, I worked in the financial and economic research industries. When the subprime mortgage housing crisis in 2008-09 sank our contry's financial system, I reinvented myself and got involved in sports analytics and the daily fantasy sports industry.

And in 2017, I moved to Deerfield Beach, Florida to pursue a real estate profession together with my sports analytics career.
